King Sherman Sauna

King Sherman Sauna is a fully licensed establishment that has been serving Hamilton’s gentlemen for over 33 years.

Our rooms are clean, nicely appointed and equipped with private showers.

If you’d like to take some additional time to relax before your rendezvous with the beautiful attendant of your choice, we offer a relaxing lounge, complementary coffee or juice, as well as a spacious dry sauna.

Enjoy your stay and unwind. Our facilities are there for you to enjoy.

Walk in clients are always welcome but if you would like to book an appointment
please call us 905-545-5030.

We are open from 10 a.m. to 1 a.m. Monday to Saturday.

Contact Us

Visit us at 893 King Street East in Hamilton. Walk in visitors are always welcome. Or feel free to call us at 905-545-5030 to book your appointment today. We are open 10 a.m. to 1 a.m. Monday to Saturday.

Parking Metered parking is available directly in front of the spa on both sides of King Street. The side streets opposite the spa also offer a discreet parking choice. Streets like St. Clair, Proctor and Holton all have parking. On some of these streets, the side available for parking will vary by the month so please check the signs posted on the street. Several parking spots are also discreetly located behind the spa. The entrance to this lot is directly beside the spa on the west side of the building. The easiest way to enter the lot is by cutting across King St. from St. Clair Ave. To see an overhead photo of the area check the Google Maps link above. If you need further information, feel free to call us at 905-545-5030.
