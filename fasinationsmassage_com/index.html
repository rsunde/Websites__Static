Fasinations Massage

Fasinations Massage is the Niagara region’s premiere body rub parlour. Come unwind with one of our gorgeous attendants. With 12 attendants to choose from, you will always find someone to suit your desires.

Fasinations is properly licensed and Niagara’s largest body rub parlour. Every room is large, clean, and well appointed with a spacious private shower. Also, all of our rooms are wheelchair accessible.

For those seeking a bit more luxury, the truly discerning visitor can enjoy the company of their beautiful attendant in our fabulous V.I.P room. Here your attendant will melt away your tensions with the help of a private jacuzzi.

A visit to Fasinations Massage is a delicious way for a gentleman to end a full day in the Falls.

We are open to serve you from 11 a.m. to 3 a.m. Sunday to Thursday. Friday and Saturday we are open from 11 a.m. to 4 a.m.
