Maxs Massage

Welcome to Max’s West Side Sauna and Massage. Max’s has been serving the Niagara region’s finest gentlemen for 17 years so you can rest assured we know how to roll out the red carpet and treat you like royalty.

At Max’s we specialize in relaxation and rejuvenation. We have 3 beautiful attendants per shift and each one of them would love to help you leave your cares behind from the moment you walk in the door.

There is no need to rush here. We strive everyday to pamper you in so many ways.

Enjoy our dry sauna and complementary beverages as a relaxing prelude to your private encounter with any of three 3 beautiful attendants per shift who would love to sooth your cares away.

You and your attendant will retire to one of our private, spacious rooms, each with its own shower and sink to enjoy a rejuvenating, sensuous, hot oil full body massage. This is where our true expertise will shine bright and warm your day.

From the moment you join us at Max’s, discretion is always assured and satisfaction is always guaranteed.

All you need to do, is experience us for yourself.

Directions

Click on “Google Maps” above to go to Google Maps. Once at the Google Maps site, there will be a box on the upper left side of the screen that says Max’s West Side Sauna and Massage. Underneath our name click on “Directions”. Then click “Get Directions” and you are done.

Don’t want to use Google’s directions? Here are the ones we’ve been using for years:

From New York State

Take thruway to Buffalo, to 190. Follow signs “To Canada/To Peace Bridge”.
Cross bridge to QEW.

Exit at Netherby Rd.

At stop sign turn left onto Netherby.

Drive 5 miles to Montrose Rd. Turn right on Montrose.

Drive 3 miles to Schisler Rd. Turn left on Schisler Rd.

Proceed on Schisler through the Tunnel and continue on East Main Street.

Cross the Lift Bridge over the canal.

Go through traffic light.

We are the second building on the right side.

From the Falls

Take the QEW Fort Erie

Exit at Lyon’s Creek Rd.

At Stop sign, turn right.

1/4 Mile to Montrose Rd.

At Montrose Rd. turn left.

Drive 2 miles to Schilser Rd.

Turn right on Schisler Rd.

Drive until you go through the tunnel and continue on East Main Street.

Cross the Lift Bridge.

Go through the traffic light.

We are the 2nd building on the right.

If you need further information, feel free to call us at (905) 714-1225.
